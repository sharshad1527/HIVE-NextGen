## **⚡ Motivation**

It started with a simple frustration: why should I have to pay a hefty monthly subscription just to use a basic glitch effect or automate my captions?  
I was tired of bloated, expensive video editing software that locked essential, everyday tools behind premium paywalls. Spending hours making repetitive cuts, applying the exact same caption styles over and over, and manually tweaking transitions was destroying my creative momentum. I just wanted to tell a story, but the software kept getting in the way.  
So, I decided to build my own.  
I built **HIVE NextGen** to fix this broken workflow. It is a streamlined, automation-first Non-Linear Editor (NLE) designed by a creator, for creators. By combining a modern, friendly UI with powerful preset-driven rendering, HIVE allows you to automate the tedious parts of editing—like dynamic captions and repetitive cuts—so you can focus entirely on your story, completely free of charge.

## **✨ Features**

Based on a robust Python and PySide6 architecture, HIVE NextGen delivers professional-grade capabilities without the clutter or the price tag:

* **🎬 Advanced Timeline & Graph Editor**: A fluid, interactive timeline (timeline\_canvas) complete with a graph editor for precise keyframe animation and easing control.  
* **🤖 Automated Captioning Engine**: Built-in support for dynamic captions. Choose from presets like *Karaoke*, *Typewriter*, *Highlight*, and *Popup* without manually setting keyframes.  
* **🎨 Preset-Driven Effects & Transitions**: Instantly apply complex visual styles (VHS, Glitch, Color Grade, Glow) and transitions (Cross Dissolve, Wipe, Slide) using our lightweight JSON preset loader.  
* **⚡ High-Performance Render Engine**: A custom backend rendering engine designed to process media assets, effects, and audio rapidly and efficiently.  
* **🖥️ Modular, Friendly UI**: A highly customizable, minimalist workspace featuring dockable panels, a dedicated project hub, and native dark mode (theme.qss).

## **🛠️ Usage**

Once the application launches, you will be greeted by the **Project Hub**.

1. **Create a New Project**: Define your aspect ratio (e.g., 16:9 for YouTube, 9:16 for Shorts/Reels) and framerate.  
2. **Import Media**: Drag and drop your raw footage into the Media Pool.  
3. **Automate Cuts & Captions**: Use the built-in automation tools in the toolbar to automatically trim silences or generate styled captions.  
4. **Apply Presets**: Drag effects from the library directly onto your clips in the timeline.  
5. **Export**: Hit the render button in the top right to compile your final video.
